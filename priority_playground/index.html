About Priority Playground

A lightweight prioritization assistant for non-technical teams.

What it does

Priority Playground helps someone take a long task list and turn it into a more realistic next step based on urgency, impact, effort, and momentum.

How it works

  • Paste one task per line.
  • Set your energy for the day.
  • The app quietly sorts the list and brings the best next tasks to the top, while still letting you override it fast.

When to use it

  • When a marketer has too many campaigns and requests competing for attention.
  • When a sales team needs to sort follow-up work, outreach, and deal tasks.
  • When an ops or leadership team needs a clearer “do now versus do later” view.
